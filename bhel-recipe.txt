Ingredients:
 - 2 cups puffed rice
 - 1/2 cup grated carrot
 - 1/2 cup chopped onions
 - 1/2 cup chopped tomatoes
 - 1 tsp green chilli paste
 - 1 tblsp olive oil
 - salt
 - Fried peanuts
 - handful chopped cilantro
 - 1 tblsp lemon juice

 Directions
 1. Heat the puffed rice in microwave for 2 minutes, for 1 minute at a time, to make it crispy
 2. Add oil, salt, chilli paste and peanuts to the puffed rice.
 3. Add the chopped onions and tomatoes, grated carrot, cilantro and mix well and serve immediately with mint and date-tamarind chutney.
